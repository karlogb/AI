# Reva Voss - Face Training Prompts (Higgsfield)

## Purpose
20 prompts for generating consistent face reference photos for Higgsfield face training.
All photos focus on FACE with clear visibility, different angles and lighting.

## Core Face Description (used in every prompt)
```
23 year old woman, silver platinum hair with dark roots, shoulder-length slightly wavy hair,
hazel-green eyes, light olive skin, light freckles across nose, silver nose stud on left nostril,
smoky eyeshadow, sharp black winged eyeliner, cat-eye, nude matte lips, natural skin texture,
highlighted cheekbones, prominent natural thick dark eyebrows, straight delicate nose, full lips
```

## Negative Prompt (use for all)
```
(deformed face:1.4), (asymmetric eyes:1.3), (bad anatomy:1.3), (blurry:1.2),
(face covered:1.4), (sunglasses:1.4), (mask:1.4), (heavy filter:1.3),
(multiple people:1.4), (cartoon:1.3), (painting:1.3), (colorful tattoos:1.3)
```

## Tips for Best Results
1. Generate each prompt multiple times and pick the most consistent face
2. Reject any where the face looks significantly different
3. Prioritize photos where freckles, nose stud, and eye color are clearly visible
4. Dark roots in the hair are important for consistency
5. The winged eyeliner is Reva's signature - make sure it's present
6. Avoid heavy filters or extreme color grading on the face
