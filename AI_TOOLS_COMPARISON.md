## Decision Criteria for Reva Voss

### Must-Haves
1. **Photo consistency** — Same face across 100+ photos (critical for believability)
2. **NSFW capability** — Fanvue is a primary revenue source
3. **Video generation** — TikTok/Reels need video content
4. **Motorcycle/automotive scenes** — Must handle vehicles in scenes

### Nice-to-Haves
1. Auto-scheduling to platforms
2. Bulk generation (batch 20+ images)
3. In-painting for fixing details
4. 4K resolution output

### Recommendation
**Start with the Budget Tier** (Foxy AI + ZenCreator):
- Use Foxy AI for all SFW Instagram/TikTok photos (best consistency)
- Use ZenCreator for NSFW Fanvue content (free credits to start)
- Upgrade to Higgsfield for video when ready
- Consider SDXL local if you want maximum control later
