# Style Guide - Reva Voss

> Style guide for consistent content generation.
> Use when creating prompts and reviewing outputs.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
| Purpose | Color | Hex | Usage |
|---------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Black | #0A0A0A | Backgrounds, vehicles, clothing |
| Secondary | Red | #CC1100 | Accent lights, helmet details |
| Accent | Green (Kawasaki) | #66CC33 | Motorcycle frame, LED accents |
| Warm | Yellow/Amber | #FFB300 | BMW angel eyes, sunset |
| Neutral | Grey/Concrete | #808080 | Garage, industrial environments |
| Skin tone | Olive | #D4A574 | Light olive with warm undertone |

### Lighting Style
- **Preferred:** Dramatic, low-key, moody lighting
- **Light sources:** Neon, LED strips, natural golden hour
- **Avoid:** Flat, even lighting; overexposed photos
- **Shadows:** Deep, contrasty - adds depth and mood

### Photography Style
- **Lens:** 85mm f/1.4 or 35mm f/1.8 equivalent
- **Depth of field:** Shallow (bokeh in background)
- **Perspective:** Eye-level or slightly low angle
- **Crop:** Portrait (4:5 for IG), landscape (16:9 for stories/banner)
- **Quality:** 8K, ultra-detailed, photorealistic

```

- In shadow / dark
- Cropped out of frame
- Too far to read
```

## Prompt Structure - Template

### Basic SFW Template
```
[QUALITY TAGS], [CAMERA/LENS], photo of Reva Voss,
[PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES], [CLOTHING], [EXPRESSION],
[ENVIRONMENT], [LIGHTING], [VEHICLE if relevant],
[COMPOSITION]

Negative: [QUALITY NEGATIVES], [LICENSE PLATE NEGATIVES],
[CHARACTER NEGATIVES - wrong eye color, wrong hair, etc.]
```

### Quality Tags (always at the beginning)
```
masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed, 8K UHD,
photorealistic, raw photo, film grain, professional photography
```

### Quality Negatives (always in negative)
```
worst quality, low quality, normal quality, lowres, blurry,
out of focus, deformed, disfigured, extra limbs, bad anatomy,
bad hands, missing fingers, watermark, text, signature
```
