## Monthly Special Content Plan

### Week 1: New Month
- Fanvue: New monthly exclusive set (10-15 photos, themed)
- IG: "Monthly recap" reel from previous month
- Subscriber voting: What they want to see this month

### Week 2: Collaboration Week
- Cross-promo with other moto/automotive accounts
- Duets on TikTok
- Guest features (if available)

### Week 3: Behind-the-Scenes
- Garage content, maintenance, detailing
- "Day in my life" longer story series
- Personal stories (Reva's backstory)

### Week 4: Premium Drop
- Best Fanvue content of the month
- IG teaser campaign (3 days before drop)
- Reddit push for traffic

## Seasonal Content

### Spring (March - May)
- "Season opener" - first ride after winter
- Bike cleaning, season prep
- Spring outfit changes (lighter jackets)
- Tatras / Slovak roads opening up

### Summer (June - August)
- Road trip content
- Sunset rides (longer days = more golden hour)
- Festival / event content
- Summer outfits, bikini + motorcycle (Fanvue)
- Highest content output of the year

### Autumn (September - November)
- Moody, atmospheric content
- Fog + motorcycle = cinematic content
- "Last rides of the season"
- Halloween special set (Fanvue)

### Winter (December - February)
- Garage season - maintenance, detailing, upgrades
- Indoor content (bedroom, bathroom, garage)
- Christmas and Valentine's special sets
- BMW M5 winter content (xDrive = driving even in winter)
- Planning and preparation for spring

## Content Batching Strategy

### Weekly Batch (Sunday or Monday)
1. Generate 15-20 photos for the entire week
2. Prepare captions for all posts
3. Schedule in scheduling tool
4. Prepare story templates

### Monthly Batch (last weekend of the month)
1. Generate Fanvue monthly set (20-30 photos)
2. Prepare 4 PPV sets
3. Create video content (4-8 reels)
4. Write captions for the entire month

## Rules

- **Never** post the same content to all platforms without adjustments
- IG and TikTok can share the same reel, but with different captions
- Fanvue content is ALWAYS exclusive - never on other platforms
- Minimum gap between posts: 3 hours
- If a viral moment happens, react immediately (interrupt the plan)
- Reddit posts always during peak US time (not EU time!)
